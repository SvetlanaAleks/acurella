Why do we need a CPA network?

CPA network is a specialized marketing platform that aims to bring together different webmasters and advertisers for mutually beneficial cooperation.

How do CPA network administrators earn money?

The platform charges the advertiser a commission for its work, it helps with the offer technical integration, searches and connects webmasters, and performs mutual settlements with them. In addition, the platform is struggling with violations and monitors the quality of traffic.

Who can use Acurella solutions?

Network administrators, webmasters, advertisers and their managers have access to the platform through two-factor authentication.

What does a web application consist of?

A typical web application consists of one page adaptive external interface and an internal interface.

How clear is the system?

All user-facing parts of the system are performance-oriented. Each feature is intuitively understandable without special training.

How secure is the platform?

All sites and public subsystems are resistant to DDoS attacks, unauthorized access, cryptographic blocking attacks, etc. Data is transmitted over secure channels (HTTPS, TLS).

Where is the data stored?

Permanent data is stored in a data storage system, temporary data (such as caches) is stored in an in-memory data store.

How stable is this storage system?

The partner network's data storage is reliable and productive. In case of any damage, the main DBMS technology is PostgreSQL. In the event of the slightest damage to your data, a backup and recovery solution will immediately work.

Can there be any problems with the access to the system?

Platform sites and subsystems are available 24/7/365. We guarantee 99.95% uptime.
