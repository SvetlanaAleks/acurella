Supply Chain Management (SCM)

Supply chain management system provides companies with state-of-the-art tools for tracking the entire production chain: from planning purchases of goods to sending orders.

Read more

All the necessary functions in one place

The system collects, stores and processes data necessary for making correct management decisions and making forecasts. Now everything is under control: the system automatically keeps records of goods, controls delivery times, helps to choose the optimal volume of purchases, plan payments, make an action plan in case of force majeure and not only.

What are SCM systems for?

SCM systems help you to manage easily the supply chains of any complexity.

How do they simplify orders management?

The platform automates the management of all stages of the company's supply and control of goods movement: purchase of raw materials, materials, production and distribution of products.

Who can use Acurella solutions?

Logisticians, commodity experts, financiers, couriers, administrators and top managers have access to the platform through two-factor authentication.

What does the system consist of?

Logistics planning and management system are built on the 1C program: Enterprise 8 with MS SQL Server as the DBMS core.

How clear is the system?

All user-facing parts of the system are performance-oriented. Each feature is intuitively understandable without special training.

How secure is the platform?

All sites and public subsystems are resistant to DDoS attacks, unauthorized access, cryptographic blocking attacks, etc. Data is transmitted over secure channels (HTTPS, TLS).

How long will the system remain up-to-date?

The expected service life of the system is at least 10 years.

How to track the effectiveness of your employees?

The system already has built-in tools for analyzing key performance indicators using customizable dashboards.

Can there be any problems with the access to the system?

Platform sites and subsystems are available 24/7/365. We guarantee 99.95% uptime.
