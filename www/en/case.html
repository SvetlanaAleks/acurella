Problem description

In order to meet the challenges of the market and to form an aggressive competitive position, Smart Media has reformed its business strategy in favor of digital transformation. With the help of Acurella solutions, the company sought to develop logistics and courier delivery. In fact, the built ecosystem helped not only to debug the delivery process of goods, but also to improve customer service, optimize marketing budgets and maintain the corporate atmosphere.

Client

Smart Media is a diversified media holding company with a large division that sells products on the Internet and wants to develop its logistics and courier delivery.

https://smartmedia.network

Solution

CRM for order management. CPA to optimize marketing budgets. SCM for planning and controlling logistics processes.
